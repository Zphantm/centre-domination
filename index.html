Centre Domination

How to Play

  • Move your pieces to adjacent empty spots.
  • Use the Centre strategically to move and control the board.
  • Capture: Jump over an opponent's piece in the centre to an opposite empty spot to remove it!
  • Win: Capture all opponent pieces or trap them so they cannot move.
